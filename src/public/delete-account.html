Delete Your Account

If you would like to permanently delete your Agri Hitech account, you can submit an account deletion request directly from the mobile app. Once approved, your account and associated data will be removed permanently.

Important Information
  • This action is permanent and cannot be undone.
  • Your profile and account-related data will be deleted.
  • Deletion requests may take up to 7 business days to process.
  • Some information may be retained temporarily for legal or security purposes.

How To Delete Your Account

  1. Open the Agri Hitech mobile app.
  2. Go to the Profile section.
  3. Open the Delete Account screen.
  4. Submit your account deletion request.

What Data Will Be Deleted?

  • User profile information
  • Business details and account preferences
  • Uploaded profile image
  • Saved account-related data

Data Retention Policy

Certain information may be retained for a limited period to comply with legal obligations, prevent fraud, resolve disputes, and maintain platform security.
